About this Generator

All athletes have to endure intense workout at the gym. Regarless of speed, length, and intensity of the workouts music is needed to fuel our motivation to keep pushing harder. This music generator takes in the length of your workout, the intensity, and a genre of music, and will output a playlist for your specific training.
